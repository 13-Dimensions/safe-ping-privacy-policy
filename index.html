SafePing – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25-November-2025

Developer: 13 Dimensions

1. Introduction

SafePing (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) is a safety-alert application designed to help users quickly send messages or emergency notifications to their selected contacts. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how SafePing collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

SafePing stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Contact name and phone number
  • Emergency contact selections
  • Message templates
  • Emergency templates

No information is uploaded, transmitted, or stored on our servers. SafePing does not create user accounts or require sign-in.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Sending Alerts and Messages

SafePing uses your locally stored contacts and templates to:

  • Send WhatsApp messages (Online Mode)
  • Send SMS messages (Offline Mode)
  • Send emergency messages to selected emergency contacts

WhatsApp messages are not sent automatically. The app opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message, and the user manually taps the Send button.

3.2 Device Permissions

To function properly, SafePing may request the following permissions:

  • SMS: To send messages in Offline Mode
  • Internet: To check Online/Offline mode and load ads
  • Network State: To automatically switch between Online and Offline modes

SafePing does not collect or upload your device contacts list. Only the contacts manually added inside the app are stored.

4. Local Storage (SQLite)

All user data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. We do not have access to this data.

You can delete all stored data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app data, or
  • Uninstalling the app

5. Third-Party Services (Ads)

SafePing uses Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial advertisements.

AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Approximate location
  • Ad performance data

This data is handled by Google according to their privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

SafePing does not collect or store this information.

6. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell user data
  • Share data with third parties
  • Transfer data outside your device
  • Use analytics, tracking, or data-mining services

All data remains on your device at all times.

7. Children’s Privacy

SafePing does not target children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If a child has entered information, uninstalling the app will remove all stored data.

8. Security

Since all data remains on your device, security is primarily handled by:

  • Your device’s operating system
  • Your screen lock, password, or security settings

SafePing does not transmit or store any personal information externally.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
